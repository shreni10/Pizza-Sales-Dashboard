# 🍕 Pizza Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of pizza sales performance. The dashboard helps identify customer preferences, peak business hours, sales trends, and top-performing pizza categories to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify peak ordering hours
- Track monthly sales trends
- Determine customer size preferences
- Discover top-selling pizzas
- Compare performance across pizza categories

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| Metric | Value |
|----------|----------|
| Total Revenue | ₹817,860.05 |
| Total Orders | 21,350 |
| Average Orders per Day | 60 |
| Average Pizzas per Order | 2.28 |

## 📈 Key Insights

### Sales Trends
- Highest monthly sales were recorded in **July (1,935 orders)**.
- Lowest monthly sales were recorded in **September (1,646 orders)**.

### Peak Ordering Hours
- **12 PM – 3 PM** is the busiest time slot with approximately **6.4K orders**.
- Evening hours (6 PM – 9 PM) also show strong customer demand.

### Busy Days
- **Friday** is the busiest day with around **3.5K orders**.
- Weekend sales contribute significantly to overall revenue.

### Customer Preferences

#### Pizza Category Distribution
- Classic: **29.02%**
- Supreme: **24.28%**
- Veggie: **23.89%**
- Chicken: **22.81%**

#### Pizza Size Distribution
- Large (L): **36.43%**
- Medium (M): **31.92%**
- Small (S): **30.01%**
- XL & XXL: Less than **2%** combined

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase promotions during lunch hours (12 PM – 3 PM) to maximize revenue.
- Focus marketing efforts on Fridays and weekends.
- Promote Large-sized pizzas as they contribute the highest share of sales.
- Leverage top-selling chicken pizzas for bundle offers and loyalty programs.
